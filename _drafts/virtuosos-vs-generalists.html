When I was a kid growing up I practiced guitar constantly. Sometimes 8 to 10 hours a day. This was the late 80's hair metal era and the time of metal guitar virtuosos. I wanted to be a virtuoso.

There are so many problems with trying being a virtuoso:

  1. It's hard.
  2. The market is very limited.
  3. ?

At the other end of the spectrum there are guitarists that have limited knowledge but much wider appeal. Angus Young has made a very good living for himself using about 3 chords.
