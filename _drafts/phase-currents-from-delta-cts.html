The sketch details the phasor math to calculate phase (line) currents given current measurements taken using delta configured CTs. I can only think of 2 reasons to use this configuration:

  1. To compensate for wye/delta phase shift. The currents are measured on the primary side of a wye/delta transformer with voltages taken on the secondary side, or vice versa.
  2. The CT secondary capacity is not adequate. Connecting the CTs in delta reduces the CT secondary current by 1.732 * ratio.
