What's up with the lack of innovation in EAF steel production? EAF steel producers used to be happy to take a small risk for a small gain. Since the crash of 2007 things have really changed. No one seems to be willing to take small risks to make small incremental improvements.

Today everyone seems to want to be paid to take no risk to be provided with low hanging fruit that will cut costs in half.
