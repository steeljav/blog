Here is Sports Illustrated's predictions for the NFL in 2006. I'm not sure that I agree at all with these picks, but they are the experts.

Divisional Playoffs:

  • AFC: Dolphins beat Steelers, Colts beat Broncos
  • NFC: Giants beat Seahawks, Panthers beat Bears

Championship Games:

  • AFC: Dolphins beat Colts
  • NFC: Panthers beat Giants

Super Bowl: Panthers 24, Dolphins 20
