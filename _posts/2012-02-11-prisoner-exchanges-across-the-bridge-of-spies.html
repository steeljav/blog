Fifty years ago yesterday Francis Gary Powers (a USAF/CIA pilot) and Rudolf Abel were exchanged between the USA and Soviet Union on the Glienicke Brucke. The History Channel has an article discussing high profile prisoner exchanges that took place on this bridge.
