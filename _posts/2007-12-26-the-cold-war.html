I finished reading The Cold War by John Lewis Gaddis last week. It was an easy read, about 350 pages. I was able to read it in my spare time over a few weeks. The book provides just the highest level details about the beginnings of the cold war after WWII up through the collapse of the Soviet Union.

If you are interested in Cold War history and want an easy read, this book is for you.
