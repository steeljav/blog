This weeks Economist magazine has an article discussing how techno clubs are declining. Kind of hard to believe for me. I went to some of these huge clubs when I was stationed in Germany in the early 90's. I didn't care for electronic music back then (I do now), but the clubs were cool. I've been told that Prague is the hotspot nowadays. I have a colleague in the EU that goes there frequently because there are more clubs, better clubs, bigger clubs etc. and it is cheap.
