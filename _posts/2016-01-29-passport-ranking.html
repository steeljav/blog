Passport Index is a site that ranks passports of the world based on the concept of a visa free score:

Visa Free Score
Passports accumulate points for each visa free country that their holders can visit without a visa, or they can obtain a visa on arrival.

Surprised to see the US and UK are tied for #1. I travel and sometimes it doesn't feel this way (thinking of you Canada).
