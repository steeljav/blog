Well, I thought that I would get a few posts up a few weeks ago, but it didn't happen. I have been very busy, and of course traveling.

I went to Brazil again for about 2 weeks. It was a really nice trip. I spent about 5 days in Curitiba which is in the state of Parana. It is a very, very nice town, and I am convinced that I could be content with living there. Unfortunately I forgot my camera cable, and so I only have a couple pictures from my colleagues phone to put up. I was able to go to another soccer game, which was awesome, and of course ate a lot of really good food. There are some outstanding restaurants in Curitiba.

I also spent 5 days on the outskirts of Sao Paulo. It was a nice trip. Very short, but nice. I would really like to spend a few years in Brazil. I think it would be a great experience.
