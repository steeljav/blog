My wife and I went out for breakfast this morning to a place in Muscatine named La Morelia. My wife had Huevos Rancheros and I had a couple Carnitas and a bowl of Menudo. The tortillas are home made, and very good. I have been there for breakfast before with some colleagues from Monterrey Mexico. They have excellent food. If you are in the area, give it a try, I highly recommend it.
