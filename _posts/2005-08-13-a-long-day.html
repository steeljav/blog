This is a picture of me at "Cola De Caballo", I think that means horse tail falls. It is about 30 miles South of Monterrey Mexico. The cost to enter is $3/person. You can drive most of the way to the falls. You have to hike about a half mile along a finished trail. There is a nice stream and picnic areas along the way.
