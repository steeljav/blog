My wife and I were able to spend Labor Day Weekend in Chicago. We stayed at the Embassy Suites in the River North area. It was a very convenient hotel to stay at. The CTA was right across the street, day passes cost $5. By using the L trains and the busses, It is very easy to get around inside of the city using the CTA. I am not sure how well it works if you are out in the suburbs though.

We spent quite a bit of time messing around at Navy Pier. This picture was taken from the pier looking West at the River North area of Chicago. I could really handle living in this part of Chicago. If you are visiting Chicago, I highly recommend that you stay in the River North area.
