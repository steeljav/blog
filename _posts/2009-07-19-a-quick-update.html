Things are going pretty well for me in Memphis. I am becoming less and less useless every day at work. I have a lot to do, and deffinitely a long way to go, but I should start making progress in the very near future.

I like the Memphis area. The food is excellent, you could really put some weight on here between Gus's Fried Chicken (probably the best I have ever had), Corky's BBQ, Interstate BBQ and all of the other great places to eat here. The traffic here is not as bad as people make it out to be.

After work Friday I went jogging along the river here. It was really nice out, sunny, not too hot, and a nice breeze off of the river. The river current is very strong down here, It is visibly strong.

Still unsure about what area to live in. I am considering Hernando Ms, and Collierville/Germantown Tn. This weekend has been beautiful, but I have been lazy and not gotten out much.

Our house in Iowa went on the market yesterday. I will really miss the house. Between working on a Masters Degree and then traveling for work I didn't have the time to do everything I wanted to it, but we did make a lot of nice improvements over the 6 years that we lived there. Someone is going to get a really nice 1918 bungalow in a great location that does not need much.
